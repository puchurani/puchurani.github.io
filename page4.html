My Dearest Bubu

We have come a long way! 

I so vividly remember our last valentine's day. Gifts had arrived and exchanged. I was saving the main one for the last. We had this feeling that, there was no proper proposal for us. That is when I knew, this has to change that Valentine's. Cut short to the main point...

I’m yours, no refunds

Evening time... you sat on the bed, I brought out the Rose (and it will never die, like our love), got to one knee and proposed to you. And as usual, you had no words to describe...and there was no need too. Your welled up eyes were saying a lot then. This whole thing is so stuck into my mind. 

I think we are suffering from a lack of Vitamin ME and YOU

And now! 

I only thing of having you by my side. Surely we have come around a very long way. We are at a good place right now. But sometimes it just kills me to not see you, hug you and kiss you. For us everyday was a hug day and kiss day, and now these days are a lot harder. But they become a lot easier whenever I see you on video call, or hear you over the normal call.

Crisis affected us too...this is our new normal :(

But i eagerly look forward to meeting you bubu. I just can't wait to be with you. I miss you a lot a lot a lot a lot...soooo damn much.

Waiting for us to meet and to be together! 

I LOVE YOU
